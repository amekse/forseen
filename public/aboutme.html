Purpose of Foresee:

The purpose of this app is to help users efficiently manage their finances by providing tools for budgeting, tracking expenses, and projecting future financial outcomes. It aims to simplify the process of creating monthly or multi-month budgets, categorizing and monitoring expenses, and understanding how their spending aligns with their financial goals. This app is designed to provide a clear view of whether your budget will be sufficient for planned purchases or if adjustments are needed.

Features of Foresee:

  1. Add Budget: Users can set a budget for a single month or across multiple months, making it easy to manage both short-term and long-term financial goals.
  2. Track Expenses: Expenses can be categorized by priority (High, Medium, Low), allowing users to track and manage their spending based on urgency and importance.
  3. Projection Insights: The app offers a projection feature that provides an overview of how your budget compares to your expenses, showing leftover budget, potential budget shortages, and a list of feasible vs. unattainable items.
  4. Budget Trend Graph: A visual representation of your monthly budget trends, helping you see how your spending evolves over time.
  5. Expense Distribution: Visual charts to break down your expenses by priority, making it easier to see where your money is going.
  6. Feasibility Analysis: The app helps users determine whether planned purchases are within their budget and provides conversion rates to show the percentage of items that can be covered by the current budget.
